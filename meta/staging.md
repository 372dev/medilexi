# Medi Lexi ŌĆö candidate queue

The public queue of terms proposed for the glossary. **A to-do list, not data.** A human refines,
translates, and validates every candidate before it becomes an entry ŌĆö this file only holds *names*
and light hints.

- **Dedup target:** `meta/all_known.txt` = every shipped `en_h` (`meta/en_h.txt`) **plus** the queued
  names below. Generated by `scripts/gen_index.ps1`; regenerate after shipping a batch or editing
  this file. A candidate is "new" only if it's absent from `all_known.txt`.
- **Machine-readable block:** the names between `QUEUE:START` and `QUEUE:END` are the parse source.
  One `- Name` per line; text before a `ŌĆö` or `(` is the name; struck/removed items are simply
  deleted, not kept.
